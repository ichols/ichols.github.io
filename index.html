International Conference
on the History of the Language Sciences

About

The International Conference on the History of the Language Sciences is a series of academic meetings focused on the historiography of linguistics and allied fields. The conference usually takes place every three years and is organised each time by a different host in their home city.

Mailing list

Participants in ICHoLS and scholars working in the history of the language sciences more broadly keep in touch over the ICHoLS mailing list. The list can be joined the following link: Subscribe ICHoLS list
